Class of 2020 B.S. in Pharmaceutical Sciences PharmDawgs,

Today, you WILL be a graduate of the University of Georgia College of Pharmacy!

To celebrate this milestone, the college will host a virtual recognition for graduates and their families, starting at 2 pm, on this Facebook page. In addition, various faculty will offer well-wishes. Tune in HERE, starting at 2 pm.

You also can watch it later on https://www.pharmdawgs.com/, along with last week's PharmD graduation and celebration! To put you in the mood for today's event, the Hugh Hodgson School of music performs UGA and graduation music! Take a listen!
