PharmD 2020 Commencement

Saturday May 2nd, 2020

Thank you for being a part of our graduates' special day and joining us as we virtually honored our student's completion of the PharmD program with the recitation of the Oath of a Pharmacist and special remarks from Provost Jack Hu, Dean Kelly Smith, Congressman Buddy Carter, as well as distinguished University of Georgia College of Pharmacy faculty and alumni.

We especially want to recognize our graduates' family & friends as well as the University of Georgia College of Pharmacy's alumni, faculty and staff for being a part of our graduates' special day.
